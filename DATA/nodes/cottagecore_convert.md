## cottagecore_convert

- **Name:** The cottagecore-to-trad convert
- **AKA:** Homesteading · back to the land · wholesome living · pastoral
- **Cluster:** lifestyle_aesthetic
- **Tags:** aesthetic, spiritual, political, community
- **Risk level:** 6
- **Entry point:** true
- **Terminal:** false
- **Target age:** 18–35
- **Target gender:** female_heavy
- **Target socioeconomic:** middle-class
- **Target psychology:** desire for simplicity, fear of modernity, need for community

**Hook:**
"Reject corporate modernity. Grow your own food. Simple living. Community."

**Cost:**
Genuine desire for simpler living gets channeled into an ethno-pastoral fantasy. 'Return to the land' becomes 'return to racial homogeneity.' The aesthetic gradually acquires political content so slowly you don't notice the shift.

**Tactics:**
- identity fusion
- isolation from support networks

**Timeline:**
- Entry: 0–12 months
- Deepening: 12–36 months
- Terminal: N/A

**Intervention:**
- Breaking: Notice when "simple living" starts excluding people based on race or background
- Alternative: Sustainable farming without ideology, diverse homesteading communities
- Resources: Permaculture groups, diverse farming cooperatives
