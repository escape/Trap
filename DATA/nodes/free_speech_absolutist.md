## free_speech_absolutist

- **Name:** The free speech absolutist
- **AKA:** Marketplace of ideas · anti-censorship · debate me
- **Cluster:** political_machines
- **Tags:** political, community, conspiracy
- **Risk level:** 6
- **Entry point:** true
- **Terminal:** false
- **Target age:** 18–40
- **Target gender:** mixed
- **Target socioeconomic:** middle-class to affluent
- **Target psychology:** desire for principle, fear of censorship, intellectual arrogance

**Hook:**
"All ideas deserve a hearing. Censorship is the real danger. Sunlight is the best disinfectant."

**Cost:**
Gets weaponized to demand platforms for content designed to dehumanize. The 'principle' is selectively applied — used to protect hate speech but rarely invoked to defend critics of the movement from harassment campaigns.

**Tactics:**
- pseudo-intellectual cover
- manufactured urgency
- us vs. them framing

**Timeline:**
- Entry: 0–6 months
- Deepening: 6–18 months
- Terminal: N/A

**Intervention:**
- Breaking: Notice the selective application of the principle; who gets silenced and who gets amplified?
- Alternative: Balanced free speech advocacy, platform accountability, constructive dialogue
- Resources: Free speech organizations with clear ethical guidelines
