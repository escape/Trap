## boss_babe

- **Name:** The boss babe / MLM recruit
- **AKA:** Network marketing · hun · girl boss · work from your phone · financial freedom for moms
- **Cluster:** hustle_wealth
- **Tags:** financial, community, spiritual
- **Risk level:** 5
- **Entry point:** true
- **Terminal:** false
- **Target age:** 25–45
- **Target gender:** female_heavy
- **Target socioeconomic:** precarious to middle-class
- **Target psychology:** financial anxiety, desire for independence, need for sisterhood

**Hook:**
"Be your own boss while raising your kids. Join a sisterhood of entrepreneurs. Financial independence on your own terms."

**Cost:**
99% of MLM participants lose money according to FTC data. The 'sisterhood' is a sales downline. You're the customer disguised as a business owner. Relationships get strip-mined for leads. The empowerment language masks a pyramid structure.

**Tactics:**
- identity fusion
- fear monetization
- isolation from support networks
- sunk-cost entrenchment

**Timeline:**
- Entry: 0–3 months
- Deepening: 3–12 months
- Terminal: N/A

**Intervention:**
- Breaking: Calculate total spend vs. total revenue, including required product purchases — the math is almost always negative
- Alternative: Genuine entrepreneurship education, women's business networks, cooperative employment models
- Resources: FTC MLM resources, SCORE women's entrepreneurship programs, Small Business Administration

**Pipeline to:** manifestation_entrepreneur, wellness_conspiracy, tradlife
