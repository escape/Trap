## edgelord

- **Name:** The edgelord / shitposter
- **AKA:** Troll · based poster · irony bro · just joking · it's a meme
- **Cluster:** online_culture
- **Tags:** political, community, isolation, violence
- **Risk level:** 6
- **Entry point:** true
- **Terminal:** false
- **Target age:** 14–25
- **Target gender:** male_heavy
- **Target socioeconomic:** mixed
- **Target psychology:** desire for attention, rebellion, testing boundaries

**Hook:**
"It's just humor. You're smarter than people who get offended. Nothing is sacred — that's freedom."

**Cost:**
Irony becomes a shield that lets genuinely hateful ideas circulate unchallenged. You can't tell where the joke ends and the belief begins — and eventually, neither can you. Researchers call this the 'visual turn' of extremism.

**Tactics:**
- irony shield
- identity fusion
- us vs. them framing

**Timeline:**
- Entry: 0–1 month
- Deepening: 1–6 months
- Terminal: N/A

**Intervention:**
- Breaking: Ask "If I wasn't joking, would I still say this?" and notice the discomfort
- Alternative: Humor communities that punch up, not down, satire with purpose
- Resources: Media literacy resources, critical thinking exercises
