## dropshipper

- **Name:** The dropshipper / course seller
- **AKA:** Digital entrepreneur · passive income · FBA guru · info product creator
- **Cluster:** hustle_wealth
- **Tags:** financial, community, political
- **Risk level:** 6
- **Entry point:** true
- **Terminal:** false
- **Target age:** 18–35
- **Target gender:** mixed
- **Target socioeconomic:** precarious
- **Target psychology:** desire for freedom, fear of employment, get-rich-quick mindset

**Hook:**
"Be your own boss. Location independence. Make money while you sleep."

**Cost:**
The product is the dream of selling the product. Most revenue comes from selling the course, not from what the course teaches. A pyramid with extra steps. The 'results' shown are the revenue from selling the course to you.

**Tactics:**
- fear monetization
- manufactured urgency
- sunk-cost entrenchment
- parasocial replacement

**Timeline:**
- Entry: 0–3 months
- Deepening: 3–12 months
- Terminal: N/A

**Intervention:**
- Breaking: Ask "Who is buying the course?" and realize the answer is "people like you"
- Alternative: Legitimate e-commerce education, small business development centers
- Resources: SCORE mentors, local SBDCs
